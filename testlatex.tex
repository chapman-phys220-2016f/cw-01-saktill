\begin{problem}{x.yz} 
Explain what the definition $f'(x)$ of a function $f(x)$ means.
\end{problem}
 
\begin{solution}
The definition of $f'(x)$ is given as
\[
\begin{split}
f'(x) =  lim_{h\to 0} frac{f(x+h) - f(x)}{h}
\end{split}
\]
